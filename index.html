About Michigan EcoData

Michigan EcoData is a home for students seeking exploration of environmental justice and ecological conservation issues through data and technology driven projects.

Members persue and design semester to year long projects that address community needs, solve environmental issues, and generate new knowledge and conversations. In addition to cutting-edge, meaningful projects, Michigan EcoData members engage in technical and professional workshops, semesterly symposiums, and social events.
